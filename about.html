About Student Finance Tracker

Welcome to the Student Finance Tracker - a simple, powerful tool designed to help students like you take control of your finances. This app was built using only Vanilla JavaScript, HTML, and CSS as a project for Frontend_Web_Development - Summative Assignment - Building Responsive UI.

Purpose

Its main goal is to help you track your daily expenses, understand your spending habits, and ensure you stay within your budget. This is especially important for students with limited resources who need to plan carefully for things like food, transport, and tuition fees.
