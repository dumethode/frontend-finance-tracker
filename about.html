About Student Finance Tracker

The Student Finance Tracker is a simple, fast, and offline-first application designed to help African Leadership University (ALU) students effectively manage their money. Built using Vanilla JavaScript, HTML, and CSS, all your data is saved securely in your web browser, ensuring it's available whenever you need it.

Mission

Our mission is to empower ALU students to take control of their finances by providing a straightforward tool for tracking expenses and staying within a set monthly budget cap. It helps visualize spending habits to make better financial decisions.

Key Features

  • Data Storage: All transactions and settings are saved directly on your device.
  • Live Dashboard: Instant summary of total spent, budget remaining, and top expense category.
  • Powerful Search: Use advanced Regular Expressions to quickly filter and find specific transactions.
  • Custom Currencies: Support for multiple base and secondary currencies, including RWF, KES, ZAR, and international standards.
